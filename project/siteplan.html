GleamsMem Studios Siteplan

Site Name

GleamsMem Studios

The name GleamsMem combines gleams (Light, something shining) and mem (memories) reflecting the studios mission to capture timeless memories.

Site Purpose

The website serves as platform for show casing their photgraphy gallery/portfolio, provide informations about their services, accepts booking and provide contact informations.

Scenarios/target audience possible questions

  • What services do you offer?
  • How much does a session cost?
  • Do I get both softcopies and hardcopies of my photos?
  • How long will it take to deliver photos after a session?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #ffd700 Gold - Used for headings, call to actions and highlights
  • Secondary Color: #141414 - Used for text and backgrounds
  • Accent Color: #f5f5f5 - Used for text and backgrounds

Typography

  • Headings Font: Playfair Display
  • Body Font: Open Sans
